⚠️ Auto Deploy is Deprecated in vSphere 9.x and will be removed in a future major release of VCF
  • Auto Deploy is deprecated in vSphere 9.x
  • Customers with local disks in their hosts must transition to Zero Touch Provisioning (ZTP) as part of this upgrade.
  • Customers with no local disks in their hosts may continue to use Auto Deploy (stateless) in vSphere 9.x, but must plan to move to Zero Touch Provisioning (ZTP).
  • Contact GSS for support with this transition: support.broadcom.com
